Chef's Choice

Pan-Seared Duck Breast

Heritage duck from Meadowbrook Farm, served with roasted butternut squash, Brussels sprouts, and pomegranate molasses reduction. Our chef sources these ducks from a family farm just 20 miles away, where they're raised on pasture with care and attention.

Farm Fresh

Heirloom Tomato & Burrata

Johnson Family Farm's prize-winning heirloom tomatoes, paired with house-made burrata, fresh basil, and aged balsamic. These tomatoes are harvested each morning at peak ripeness, just 15 miles from our kitchen.

Artisanal Cheese Selection

Chef's Pick

Pan-Seared Scallops

Day-boat scallops seared to perfection, served over cauliflower puree with crispy pancetta and microgreens. Fresh from the Atlantic coast, delivered daily.

Main Courses

Signature

Herb-Crusted Rack of Lamb

New Zealand lamb with rosemary-thyme crust, served with roasted fingerling potatoes and seasonal vegetables. Our signature preparation showcases the natural flavors with a perfect herb balance.

Atlantic Salmon
